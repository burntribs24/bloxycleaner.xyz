How It Works

Bloxy Cleaner is a Discord Bot that provides lists of users that are in, or related to, Roblox Condo communities. A condo community is a server where people go to find Roblox related NSFW games and or media.

Bloxy Cleaner provides a continuously updated database of users who are actively involved in said Condo communities. We have been collecting and verifying this data through multiple private sources to ensure accuracy. There is no false detections. Every user in the database has been confirmed to be apart of these communities, even if they have never sent a message.

When someone joins your discord server, the bot will instantly check if they are in a condo server. If they are, they will be banned so they cannot cause harm to your server.

This project is heavily inspired by Ruben Sim's famous Ro-Cleaner.
